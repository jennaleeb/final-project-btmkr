# Picknic

Food is better with friends.

Find something that fits everyone's dietary preferences.

About

Picnic is a social app that let's you set your dietary preferences and share them with your friends. As a host, it makes sure you have something for everyone. As a guest, dynamically update your status and let your host know what's on and off the table.
