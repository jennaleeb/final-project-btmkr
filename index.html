What is Picknic?

Picknic is a social app that let's you set your dietary preferences and share them with your friends. As a host, it makes sure you have something for everyone. As a guest, dynamically update your status and let your host know what's on and off the table.

Background

The number of people living with food sensitivities has risen over 20% in the last 10 years.

The Challenge

Finding a meal to fit everyone's unique needs, that seem to be constantly changing!

How it Works

Use Picknic to set up your personalized dietary profile share it with your friends and family.
